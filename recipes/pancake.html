Blueberry Pancake

Descripton: this pancake is golden and lightly crispy at the edges with a soft, tendre center. Blueberry pancakes with fresh blueberries are most definitely not just for any occassion. The baking powder in this pancake recipe is what makes them so fluffy!. its the yummmiest thing on earth.Its made up od milk, flour, baking powder, salt, milk.

Ingredients

  • 11/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 11/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 12 cup frozen blueberries, thawed

Directions

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and sugar, and salt together in a bowl. Combine milk and egg in a small bowl. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Stir in melted butter, then fold in blueberries. Set aside for 1 hour.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or nonstick frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour or scoop the batter onto the hot griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
