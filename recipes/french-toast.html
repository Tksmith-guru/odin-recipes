Blueberry French Toast

Description: the blueberry french toast casserole is a very unique breakfast disk. Good for any holidday breakfast or brunch, it's filled with the fresh taste of blueberrries and covered with a rich blueberry sauce to make it look yummy and delicious to eat. Its one of a kind, if you are someone looking for something to that willl make you to savour the taste. Then, this dish is the one for you. its made up of a slice of bread, cream cheese, fresh blusberries, egg, milk, vanilla extract (optional) and syrup.

Ingredients for French Toast

  • 12 slices day-old braed, cut into i-inch cube
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, cut into 1 inch cube
  • i cup fresh blueberries
  • 12 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup

Ingredients for blueberry Sauce

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  • Prepare casserole; Lightly grease a 9x13 - inch baking dish. Arrange 1/2 of the bread cubes in the dish and top with cream cheese cube. Sprinkle blueberries over the cream cheese, then top with remaining bread cubes.
  • Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and syrup together in a large bowl until well-combined: pour over the bread cubes. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  • Remove casserole from the Refrigerator about 30 minutes before baking. Preheat the oven to 350 degreesF (175 degrees C).
  • Bake cassserole in the preheated oven, covered for 30 minutes. Uncover, and continue baking until the center is firm and surface is lightly browned, about 25 to 30 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, preparae blueberry sauce: Mix sugar, water, and cornstarch together in a medium saucepan; bring to a boil and cook, stiring constantly, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in blueberries, reduce heat to low, and simmer until all the blueberries burst, about 10 miiutes. Stir in buter.
  • Serve portions of casserole on plate and pour warm syrup over the top.
